Our Story

A New Standard
For Sports in Salem

Vadivasal Turf was born from a simple belief: every athlete, amateur or elite, deserves a world-class stage to perform. We set out to create not just a sports facility, but an experience that elevates your game.

Nestled in Salem, Tamil Nadu, our premium grounds offer state-of-the-art infrastructure, meticulous maintenance, and an atmosphere that brings out the champion in you.
